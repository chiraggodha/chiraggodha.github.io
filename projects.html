Maze Searching Techniques

In order to analyze different path finding algorithms, I created a simulation to test for the best path finding solutions on a given problem. The simulation was set up with an initial generation of blocked and open cells on a NxN grid, 'spaceship'. There exists a bot, button, and fire on three random locations of the spaceship and the goal is for the bot to reach the button before the fire spreads and destroys the button or bot. I created 4 different versions of this bot to simulate different strategies and tested to see which was the most efficient. Bot1 was a simple DFS implementation which failed more often than not. Bot2 utilized BFS which proved to be substantially more successful than Bot1. Bot3 used a modified version of BFS with a priority queue and fire spread probability information to try and beat the spread of the fire. Bot4 used A* and used the Manhattan distance to the button as its heuristic.
